Working Prototype · Evidence Synthesis Engine

Creative Intelligence System

CIS synthesizes the creative-effectiveness evidence you already collect but rarely connect — emotional measures, creative attributes, attention, consumer verbatims, brand impact, and business outcomes — into one read: Evidence → Interpretation → Recommendation. It organizes everything as a chain — Creative Evidence → Brand Evidence → Business Evidence — and finds where it breaks down.

The campaigns are real, recognizable ads; the measures mirror the Ace Metrix / iSpot model (Likeability, emotional metrics, attributes vs. category norm, brand recall, intent, outcomes). Signal values here are illustrative reconstructions drawn from each ad's public reputation — not actual Ace Metrix scores — and gaps are shown honestly as "Evidence Unavailable."
