Working Prototype · Evidence-Based Decision Layer

Creative Intelligence System

CIS is not another dashboard. It reads the evidence your existing tools already produce, works out where performance is breaking down, and recommends what to do next — with the rationale in plain language. The scoring still happens underneath (tap any signal, or open Under the hood), but the recommendation is the product and the evidence is the rationale.

The 12 campaigns are derived from a real public Facebook-ads dataset. Behavioral & Business reads come from the actual metrics (impressions, clicks, spend, conversions, CPA); the upper-funnel reads (Context, Attention, Creative) are inferred from engagement and marked lower-confidence, since no public source measures them.
