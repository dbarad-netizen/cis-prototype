Working Prototype · Evidence Synthesis Platform

Creative Intelligence System

CIS is not another dashboard or another score. It synthesizes the evidence you already collect but rarely connect — quantitative signals and the actual language consumers use — works out where performance is breaking down, and recommends what to do next. Every read follows the same chain: Evidence → Interpretation → Recommendation. The scoring still happens underneath (open Under the hood), but the recommendation is the product and the evidence is the rationale.

The 12 campaigns are derived from a real public Facebook-ads dataset; Behavioral & Business reads come from the actual metrics, while the upper-funnel reads (Context, Attention, Creative) are inferred and marked lower-confidence. The consumer verbatims are illustrative placeholders for real Ace Metrix open-ends — they show how qualitative evidence sits alongside the numbers, and where the two agree or conflict.
